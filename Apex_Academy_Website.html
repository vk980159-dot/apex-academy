FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process? ▼
Students must fill the online admission form, submit documents, and attend an orientation test. Shortlisted students get a counselling call.
Is there an EMI option for fees? ▼
Yes! We offer 0% interest EMI plans. Fees can be paid in 3–6 monthly installments with post-dated cheques or online payment.
Do you provide study material? ▼
Yes, comprehensive printed study material is included in all courses. Additional PDFs and videos are available in the student portal.
Are there scholarship programs? ▼
We offer merit scholarships up to 100% fee waiver based on our scholarship entrance test held every quarter.
What is the batch size? ▼
We maintain small batch sizes of 30–45 students to ensure personalized attention. Every student gets adequate teacher interaction.
Do you offer online classes? ▼
Yes! Our full-featured online program includes live classes, recorded lectures, digital notes, doubt sessions, and online tests.
